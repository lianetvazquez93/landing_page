Preparation example

Arroz con leche

It is a dessert that is made by cooking the rice with milk, sugar and a cinnamon stick until it is very soft and creamy. It is consumed cold and in one of its Creole versions it can be prepared adding to it fresh shredded coconut.

Flan de leche condensada

To a can of condensed milk add a cup of evaporated milk, 5 eggs and a teaspoon of vanilla, beat well and the mixture is deposited in a container whose interior has been previously covered with a caramel. Cover with baking paper and cook in a bain-marie for 1 hour (30 minutes if under pressure).Allow to cool at least 2 hours before unmolding.

Dulce de leche cortada

If the milk was cut, do not throw it away! Add a good amount of sugar and let the mixture boil until it thickens. If you add a piece of lemon or orange rind, the result will be even better. An exquisite sweet that you will surely have to repeat.
